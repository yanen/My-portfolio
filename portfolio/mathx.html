Mobile App MathX

Math notes app

A math notes app for your upcoming tests.

Find it on the IOS app store.

Overview

It provides free online math notes, mainly catered towards SST students. It also comes along with some useful math tools like a Matrix multiplication calculator.

How was I involved?

I am the lead developer of this application and coded out the functionalities of this app using Xcode and the Swift programming language.

Developement proccess

The app first came about as we saw the hassle of reading cumbersome notes on public transportation and the lack of any free offline mathematics notes app without advertisements.

Our company engaged with the Math Teachers of SST to find out what features they needed and after months of development, finally released the first version of MathX in 2018.

We are planning to release a new update later this year, pushing out new notes for the Secondary three and Secondary four levels and a revamped UI.
